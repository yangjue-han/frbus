2018 Version of the FRB/US Model


Overview of the Changes to the FRB/US Model

Simplification in modeling energy and the supply-side

  • Petroleum imports (EMP)
  • Business Potential Output (XGPOT and XBT)

Simplification of Business Investment

Simplification of the government sector

  • Federal Government Budget
  • State and Local Government Expenditures
  • Federal Government Expenditures
  • Government Transfer Payments
  • Personal Income Tax and Non-Tax Receipts
  • Corporate Income Tax

Miscellaneous simplifications and changes

  • Imports excluding Petroleum (EMO and PMO)
  • Household Permanent Income Components
  • Desired level of consumption (QEC)
  • PICNIA: Inflation rate, personal consumption expenditures
  • Corporate profits (YNICPN)
  • Consumption of fixed capital (JCCAN)

Simplification of aggregation in the model

Elimination of compounded annualized rates
