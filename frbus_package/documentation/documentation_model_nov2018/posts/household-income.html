Household Permanent Income Components

Changes to the FRB/US Model

YHGAP: Income, household, total, ratio to XGDP, cyclical component
Old model 
yhgap - yhgap_aerr = 100*(yhshr/zyhst - 1)
New model 
yhgap - yhgap_aerr = 100*log(yhshr/zyhst)
YHTGAP: Income, household, transfer, ratio to YH, cyclical component
Old model 
yhtgap - yhtgap_aerr = 100*(yhtshr/zyhtst - 1)
New model 
yhtgap - yhtgap_aerr = 100*log(yhtshr/zyhtst)

Some Details of the Simplifications

  • A slightly different convention has been adopted for the “permanent income” group of variables.

  • We have replaced the percentage deviation convention by a log change approximation. This change facilitates the automation of the linearization of FRB/US.

  • The equations of the trend shares were respecified accordingly.

ZYHST: Expected trend ratio of household income to GDP
ZYHPST: Expected trend share of property income in household income
ZYHTST: Expected trend share of transfer income in household income
Old model 
zyhst - zyhst_aerr = zyhst(-1) + y_zyhst(1)*(yhshr - zyhst(-1))
zyhpst - zyhpst_aerr = zyhpst(-1) + y_zyhpst(1)*(yhpshr - zyhpst(-1))
zyhtst - zyhtst_aerr = zyhtst(-1) + y_zyhtst(1)*(yhtshr - zyhtst(-1))
New model 
log(zyhst) - zyhst_aerr = log(zyhst(-1)) + y_zyhst(1)*yhgap(-1)/100
log(zyhpst) - zyhpst_aerr = log(zyhpst(-1)) + y_zyhpst(1)*yhpgap(-1)/100
log(zyhtst) - zyhtst_aerr = log(zyhtst(-1)) + y_zyhtst(1)*yhtgap(-1)/100
