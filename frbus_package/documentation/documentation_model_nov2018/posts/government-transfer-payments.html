Government Transfer Payments

Changes to the FRB/US Model

  • Federal and state and local (S&L) government net transfer payments (GFTN and GSTN) were consolidated into a single government transfer net payments variable (GTN).
Old model 
gftn - gftn_aerr = .01*pgdp*gft
gftn - gftn_aerr = .01*pgdp*gft
New model 
gtn - gtn_aerr = .01*pgdp*gtr
  • Real (deflated by PGDP) federal and S&L government net transfer payments (GFT and GST) were consolidated into a single government transfer net payments variable (GTR).
Old model 
gft - gft_aerr = (gftrd + gftrt)*xgdpt
gst - gst_aerr = (gstrd + gstrt)*xgdpt
New model 
gtr - gtr_aerr = (gtrd + gtrt)*xgdpt
  • The deviation of the ratios of federal and S&L transfers to GDP from their trend ratios were replaced by a single and consolidated variable, GTRD.
Old model 
gftrd - gftrd_aerr = 
y_gftrd(1)
 + y_gftrd(2)*gftrd(-1)
 + y_gftrd(3)*xgap2
gstrd - gstrd_aerr = 
y_gstrd(1)
 + y_gstrd(2)*gstrd(-1)
 + y_gstrd(3)*xgap2
New model 
gtrd - gtrd_aerr = 
y_gtrd(1)*gtrd(-1)
 + y_gtrd(2)*xgap2

The eoxgenous variables GFTRT and GSTRT (Federal and State/Local government, trend ratio of transfer payments to GDP) have been combined to become GTRT.

Some details of the changes and simplifications

  • We have simplified and reduce the number of variables forming the government transfer payment block through the consolidation of the federal and S&L variables.
