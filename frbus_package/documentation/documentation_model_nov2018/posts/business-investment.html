Business Investment

Changes to the FRB/US model

Business investment

  • We consolidated three different business investment variables (EPD, EPS, EPI) into a single business fixed investment variable (EBFI). This page only shows the changes relative to EPD for simplicity.
Old model 
dlog(epd) - epd_aerr = 
(y_epd(1)*(log(qepd(-2)/epd(-2)))
 + y_epd(2)* dlog(epd(-1))
 + y_epd(3)* dlog(epd(-2)) + zxbd(-1) + zvpd(-1))*(1
 - y_epd(4))
 + y_epd(4)*(dlog(xbo(-1)) + hgvpd(-1))
epdn - epdn_aerr = 0.01*ppdr*pxp*epd
New model 
dlog(ebfi) - ebfi_aerr = 
(y_ebfi(1)*(log(qebfi(-1)/ebfi(-1)))

 + y_ebfi(3)*dlog(ebfi(-2))
 + y_ebfi(4)*zebfi)*(1
 - y_ebfi(5))
 + y_ebfi(5)*(dlog(xb(-1)) - hgpbfir(-1)/400)
ebfin - ebfin_aerr = 0.01*ebfi*pbfir*pxp
  • We consolidated the relative price variables (e.g. PPDR), trend growth rate (e.g. HGVPD), and target level (e.g. QEPD).

Capital stock

  • Variables relating to business capital were simplified to reflect the aggregate representation of business capital: capital stock equations (KPD), depreciation rates (JRPD), multiplicative factor (UPKPD), price ratio (PKPDR), and income from capital stocks (YKPDN)
Old model 
kpd - kpd_aerr = 0.25*epd + (1 - jrpd/4)*kpd(-1)
pkpdr - pkpdr_aerr = upkpd*ppdr
ykpdn - ykpdn_aerr = .01*rtpd*pxb*(kpd + kpd(-1))/2
New model 
kbfi - kbfi_aerr = (pbfir/pkbfir)*ebfi/4 + (1 - jrbfi/4)*kbfi(-1)
pkbfir - pkbfir_aerr = upkbfir*pbfir
ykbfin - ykbfin_aerr = .01*rtbfi*pxb*(kbfi + kbfi(-1))/2
  • Exogenous factors were created for the fixed business investment series: the depreciation rate has become JRBFI and the multiplificative factor associated with the relative price of capital has become UPKBFIR.

User costs and capital taxes

  • The user costs and tax rates that previously applied to the three different types of capital now apply to the aggregate capital measure. These include the depreciation allowance (TAPDD), investment tax credit (TAPDT), after tax cost of capital (RPD), user cost of capital (RTPD), and the target capital to output ratio that depends on these tax rates (VPD).

*((pxp*pkpdr + pxp(-1)*pkpdr(-1))/2)/pxb

rbfi - rbfi_aerr = 0.5*(7.2 + (1 - trfcim)*(rg5 + rbbb - rg10) - zpib5)

*((1 - .01*tritc - trfcim*(1 - tapddp*.01*tritc)*tdpv)/(1 - trfcim))

  • The present value of depreciation allowances for BFI is represented by the exogenous factor (TDPV) and the investment tax credit for BFI by the exogenous factor (TRITC).
