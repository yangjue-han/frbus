Desired level of consumption (QEC)

Changes to the FRB/US Model

  • The linear relationship between the desired level of consumption and permanent income and wealth was replaced by a log-log specification. As in the previous model, the MPC is the same for both types of wealth. As a result, stock market wealth (WPS) and other wealth (WPO) are aggregated. The log-log specification has been adopted for consistency with spending variables in other equations in the model and to accelerate convergence dynamics following transient shocks.
